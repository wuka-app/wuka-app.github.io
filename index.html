Features

Check out what you can do with this app!

Mobility

How to keep training process when you go somewhere far away from your gym? Wuka is always with you. It's like your personal pocket coach.

Flexibility

No matter what your are doing(IronMan, CrossFit, Marathons, Yoga, Freediving, Football, etc) You are free to make any kind of workouts for you.

Artificial trainer

Forget about handwritten lists of workouts, youtube videos and timers that you need to use together. Concentrate on your workout all the rest wuka will do for you.

Socialization

Find people who are also inspired by the sport like you. Share your workouts and records. Find new ways to train yourself.
